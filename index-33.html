Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026. This policy explains what Jarvis AI Pro does and does not do with your information.

What we collect

  • Account info: your name, email, and profile picture (via Google Sign-In or email/password)
  • Your chat messages and generated images, so your history syncs across your devices
  • Basic usage stats (message counts) stored on your device

What we do with it

  • Store your chats securely in Firebase so you can access them later or on another device
  • Send your messages to AI providers (Groq) solely to generate a reply to you
  • Nothing is sold. Nothing is shared with advertisers.

What we don't do

  • We don't read your chats for advertising or profiling
  • We don't share your personal data with third parties beyond what's needed to generate AI replies (Groq) or generate images (a public image API)
  • We don't sell your data to anyone
  • Voice recognition and text-to-speech run in your browser — audio is not stored on our servers

Your choices

  • You can delete individual chats anytime from History
  • You can log out anytime from Profile, which ends your session on this device
  • Uploaded files/images are processed to generate a response and are not permanently stored beyond your chat history

Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised via the app's feedback channel.
